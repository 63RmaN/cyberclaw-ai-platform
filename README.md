# CyberClaw

## AI Automation & Cybersecurity Solutions Platform

CyberClaw is an AI-powered business automation platform designed to help organizations improve productivity, reduce operational costs, and strengthen cybersecurity through intelligent AI agents.

The goal of CyberClaw is to create reusable AI solutions that can be customized for different industries and business needs.

---

# Vision

Build a modular AI platform that allows companies to deploy specialized AI assistants for their daily operations.

Instead of businesses building AI solutions from scratch, CyberClaw provides ready-to-customize AI agents.

---

# Core AI Agents

CyberClaw will develop reusable AI agents including:

- AI Receptionist
- AI Customer Support Agent
- AI Sales Assistant
- AI HR Assistant
- AI Finance Assistant
- AI Document Assistant
- AI Knowledge Assistant
- AI Help Desk Agent
- AI Cybersecurity Assistant
- AI Compliance Assistant
- AI Reporting Assistant
- AI Voice Assistant
